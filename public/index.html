Games

  • Get 10 products by page number: /games/?page=1 (GET)
  • Get X products per page: (default: 9) /games/?perPage=10 (GET)
  • Search product by name or information /games/search/?s=metroid (GET)
  • Get proudct by Category/Name: /games/category/name (GET)
  • Price Range /games/prices/?max=x&min=y&page=z (GET)
